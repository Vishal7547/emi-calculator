what is lumpsum calculator ?

A lump sum calculator is a utility tool that shows you the wealth gained over the long-term. It is a smart tool to calculate the return on a lump-sum mutual fund investment.

what is lumpsum calculator formula ?

formula is F.V:-FV = PV(1+R)^N

where

F.V:FUTURE VALUE

P: principal amount

R: rate if interest

N: Loan tenure

EXAMPLE OF LUMPSUM CALCULATOR.

Rajeev invested a lump sum amount of Rs 1,00,000 in a mutual fund scheme for 20 years. Rajeev has the expected rate of return of 10% on the investment. rajeev may calculate the future value of the investment as

FV = 1,00,000(1+0.1)^20 FV = Rs 6,72,750

rajeev has invested 1,00,000

which has grown to rs 6,72,750

wealth gain is Rs 6,72,750 - Rs 1,00,000 = Rs 5,72,750.

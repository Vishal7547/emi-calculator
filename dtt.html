what is home loan ?

A home loan is a secured loan that is obtained to purchase a property by offering it as collateral

what is home loan emi calculations formula ?

formula is EMI: [P x R x (1+R)^N]/[(1+R)^N-1]

where

EMI:equated monthly installment

P: principal amount

R: rate if interest

N: Loan tenure
